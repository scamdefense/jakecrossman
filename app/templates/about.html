Jacob "Jake" Crossman

Jacob "Jake" Crossman is an actor and digital creator splitting time between Los Angeles and his quiet state of Virginia. A natural athlete with strong comedic instincts, Jake first made his mark on ESPN+'s sports-comedy series FUSE and most recently was featured in the indie pilot Continue to Win directed by Lester Speight. He also consulted on the feature film F1 The Movie directed by Joseph Kosinski, lending his cardistry expertise on location in Northamptonshire, England.

Trained in classical theatre and featured as Chaucer in The Canturbury Tales and Fagin in Oliver Twist at Erie's Theater, he combines physicality, improvisation chops, and a magnetic presence to bring characters vividly to life. Off-camera, he leverages over 1 million TikTok followers to craft brand-driven health and wellness content with 250 million+ views.

Jake's versatility spans from dramatic lead roles to sketch comedy, showcasing his range and dedication to the craft. His athletic background provides a unique physicality to his performances, while his digital media experience brings a contemporary edge to traditional acting.
