Jacob "Jake"Crossman

Jacob "Jake" Crossman is an actor and digital creator splitting time between Los Angeles and his home state of Virginia. A natural athlete with strong comedic instincts, Jake first made his mark on ESPN+'s sports-comedy series Fuse and most recently headlined the indie pilot Continue to Win.

Trained in classical theatre—originating Fagin in Oliver Twist at Erie's Warner Theater—he combines physicality, improvisation chops, and a magnetic presence to bring characters vividly to life. Off-camera, he leverages over 1 million TikTok followers to craft brand-driven health and wellness content with 250 million+ views.

Jake's versatility spans from dramatic lead roles to sketch comedy, showcasing his range and dedication to the craft. His athletic background provides a unique physicality to his performances, while his digital media experience brings a contemporary edge to traditional acting.
